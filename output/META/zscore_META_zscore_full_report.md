## Z-Score Formula Used

Z = 6.56*X1 + 3.26*X2 + 6.72*X3 + 1.05*X4
- X1 = (Current Assets - Current Liabilities) / Total Assets
- X2 = Retained Earnings / Total Assets
- X3 = EBIT / Total Assets
- X4 = Equity / Total Liabilities

**Thresholds:**
- Safe Zone: > 2.90
- Grey Zone: > 1.23 and <= 2.90
- Distress Zone: <= 1.23

## 1. Diagnostic Evaluation of Financial Health

Meta Platforms, Inc. currently operates within the **Safe Zone** of the Altman Z-Score framework, with a Z-Score of **16.748** as of Q1 2025. This indicates a strong financial position, characterized by robust liquidity, profitability, capital efficiency, and low leverage.

- **Liquidity:** The company's current assets significantly exceed its current liabilities, indicating a healthy liquidity position. The X1 ratio of **0.201** reflects a solid buffer against short-term obligations.
  
- **Profitability:** Meta's retained earnings (X2) at **0.362** demonstrate a strong capacity for reinvestment and growth, showcasing the company's ability to generate profits over time.

- **Capital Efficiency:** The EBIT to total assets ratio (X3) of **0.066** indicates that Meta is effectively utilizing its assets to generate earnings, although there is room for improvement.

- **Leverage:** The equity to total liabilities ratio (X4) of **13.147** highlights the company's low reliance on debt, suggesting a conservative approach to financing that minimizes financial risk.

Overall, the Z-Score trajectory shows consistent strength, with no significant fluctuations or concerns noted in the diagnostic evaluation.
