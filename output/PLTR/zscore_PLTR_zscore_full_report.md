## Z-Score Formula Used

Z = 6.56*X1 + 3.26*X2 + 6.72*X3 + 1.05*X4
- X1 = (Current Assets - Current Liabilities) / Total Assets
- X2 = Retained Earnings / Total Assets
- X3 = EBIT / Total Assets
- X4 = Equity / Total Liabilities

**Thresholds:**
- Safe Zone: > 2.90
- Grey Zone: > 1.23 and <= 2.90
- Distress Zone: <= 1.23

## 1. Diagnostic Evaluation of Financial Health

### Liquidity
Palantir's liquidity position is strong, as indicated by a current ratio (X1) of approximately 0.79 in the latest quarter. This suggests that the company has sufficient current assets to cover its current liabilities, although it is slightly below the ideal threshold of 1.0.

### Profitability
The company's profitability is reflected in its retained earnings (X2), which are negative, indicating that Palantir has accumulated losses over time. This is a concern, as it may affect investor confidence and the company's ability to reinvest in growth.

### Capital Efficiency
Palantir's EBIT (X3) is modest, showing a slight positive return on total assets. This indicates that while the company is generating some earnings, it may not be fully capitalizing on its asset base.

### Leverage
The leverage ratio (X4) is exceptionally high at 156.81, suggesting that the company has a significant amount of equity relative to its liabilities. This is a positive indicator, as it implies a lower risk of insolvency.

### Z-Score Trajectory and Risk Status
Palantir's Z-Score has consistently remained in the Safe Zone, with the latest score at 167.598. This indicates a low risk of bankruptcy and a stable financial position. However, the negative retained earnings signal a need for improvement in profitability to sustain long-term growth.
