## Z-Score Formula Used

Z = 6.56*X1 + 3.26*X2 + 6.72*X3 + 1.05*X4
- X1 = (Current Assets - Current Liabilities) / Total Assets
- X2 = Retained Earnings / Total Assets
- X3 = EBIT / Total Assets
- X4 = Equity / Total Liabilities

- Safe Zone: > 2.90

The profitability metrics, represented by the X3 component, have also shown a negative trend, particularly in Q1 2024 where it dropped to -0.112. This indicates that the company is facing challenges in generating earnings before interest and taxes relative to its total assets, which is alarming for a firm in the real estate industry.

The X2 component, which measures retained earnings relative to total assets, has fluctuated around zero, indicating that Cresud is not effectively reinvesting its profits back into the business. This could hinder long-term growth and sustainability.

### Leverage
The X4 component, representing equity relative to total liabilities, is currently at zero, suggesting that the company may be heavily leveraged. This lack of equity cushion increases financial risk, especially in a downturn.

### Z-Score Trajectory and Risk Status
Cresud's Z-Score has consistently fallen within the Distress Zone, with a score of 0.122 in Q4 2024.
