## Z-Score Formula Used

Z = 6.56*X1 + 3.26*X2 + 6.72*X3 + 1.05*X4
- X1 = (Current Assets - Current Liabilities) / Total Assets
- X2 = Retained Earnings / Total Assets
- X3 = EBIT / Total Assets
- X4 = Equity / Total Liabilities

- Safe Zone: > 2.90

## 1. Diagnostic Evaluation of Financial Health

Baidu's financial health can be evaluated through key metrics of liquidity, profitability, capital efficiency, and leverage:

- **Liquidity**: The company's current ratio, represented by X1, is 0.205, indicating a potential liquidity concern as it suggests that current liabilities exceed current assets.
- **Profitability**: The EBIT to total assets ratio (X3) is 0.017, reflecting low profitability relative to total assets, which may signal challenges in generating sufficient operational income.
- **Capital Efficiency**: The retained earnings ratio (X2) at 0.421 shows that Baidu has a reasonable level of retained earnings, indicating some degree of capital efficiency.
- **Leverage**: The equity to total liabilities ratio (X4) is 0.163, suggesting that the company is highly leveraged, which can increase financial risk.

Based on the Altman Z-Score of 3.004, Baidu falls into the **Safe Zone**. This indicates a low risk of bankruptcy and suggests that the company is financially healthy. However, the liquidity and profitability metrics indicate areas for improvement.
