## Z-Score Formula Used

Z = 6.56*X1 + 3.26*X2 + 6.72*X3 + 1.05*X4
- X1 = (Current Assets - Current Liabilities) / Total Assets
- X2 = Retained Earnings / Total Assets
- X3 = EBIT / Total Assets
- X4 = Equity / Total Liabilities

**Thresholds:**
- Safe Zone: > 2.90
- Grey Zone: > 1.23 and <= 2.90
- Distress Zone: <= 1.23

## 1. Diagnostic Evaluation of Financial Health

### Liquidity
Shopify's liquidity position is strong, as indicated by the X1 component of the Z-Score, which measures the company's ability to cover its short-term liabilities with its current assets. The X1 value of 0.406 in Q1 2025 suggests that Shopify has sufficient liquidity to meet its obligations.

### Profitability
The profitability of Shopify is reflected in the X3 component, which measures earnings before interest and taxes (EBIT) relative to total assets. The current X3 value of 0.015 indicates that while the company is generating some earnings, there is room for improvement in operational efficiency.

### Capital Efficiency
The X2 component, representing retained earnings as a percentage of total assets, shows a value of 0.071. This suggests that Shopify has retained a portion of its earnings, which is a positive indicator of capital efficiency, although the low value indicates potential challenges in reinvesting profits for growth.

### Leverage
Shopify's leverage is assessed through the X4 component, which measures equity relative to total liabilities. With an X4 value of 49.514, the company demonstrates a strong equity position, indicating low financial risk and a solid capital structure.

### Z-Score Trajectory and Risk Status
Shopify's Z-Score has consistently remained in the Safe Zone, with a score of 54.983 in Q1 2025. This indicates a strong financial health status, suggesting that the company is not at risk of bankruptcy and is well-positioned for growth.
